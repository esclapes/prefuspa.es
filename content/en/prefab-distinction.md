## Wood Frame

They represent the perfect combination of the natural quality of wood with its durability and versatility, giving a distinctive touch to each project.

Each wood frame is meticulously designed and manufactured, based on extensive experience in the construction industry, ensuring solidity and safety for your home. This option is ideal for those who value a traditional approach with a classic aesthetic and the possibility of achieving exceptional customisation.

## Straw Modules

At the forefront of sustainable construction. Each module is meticulously designed to minimise environmental impact and maximise energy efficiency, representing an eco-friendly solution of high quality and reliability. If you are looking for an innovative alternative committed to protecting the planet, they are the perfect choice to make your vision of a sustainable home a reality.
